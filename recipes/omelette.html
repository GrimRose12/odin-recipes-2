Omelette

Description

One of the basic egg dishes. The only way to ruin it is by burning it. Has many variations.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green onion
  • Seasonings (Optional)
    • Salt
    • MSG
    • Soy sauce
    • Dashi
    • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Break the egg, and whisk
  2. Chop tomatoes and onion into small dices
  3. Mix the chopped ingredients and the egg mixture
  4. Fry for 5-6 minutes, until the omelette is nice and firm
  5. Chop and add green onions as garnish
  6. Add your preferred seasonings
