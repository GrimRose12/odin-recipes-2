Fried Egg

Description

One of the basic egg dishes. Drowning it in oil is the best way.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 3 chillies
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green onion
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings (Optional)
    • Salt
    • MSG
    • Soy sauce
    • Dashi

Steps

  1. Pour enough oil where you can drown your egg
  2. Break the egg, and drop the white and yolk onto the pan
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to overcook the yolk, make sure it remains slimy. Cook until you see a good amount of golden brown edges
  4. Take the fried egg, sprinkle with chopped chillies
