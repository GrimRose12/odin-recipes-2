Tamago Kake Gohan

Description

An egg on warm rice. A basic Japanese egg dish. Simple, but delicious. My go-to comfort food when I'm lazy.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of rice
  • Seasonings (Optional)
    • Salt
    • MSG
    • Soy sauce
    • Dashi

Steps

  1. Cook rice, then place it in a cup
  2. While the rice is still hot, pour the raw egg on it
  3. Mix well
  4. Add seasonings
